Steak Tacos

description:

A perfectly grilled steak taco with tender, juicy meat, topped with fresh cilantro, zesty onions, a squeeze of lime, and a smoky chipotle salsa. Wrapped in a warm, slightly charred corn tortilla, it's a burst of savory, spicy, and refreshing in every bite.

Ingredients:

  • Steak
  • Tortillia
  • Onion
  • Cilantro

Steps:

  1. Cook the Steak: Heat a skillet or grill over medium-high heat. Cook your steak (any cut you like) for about 3-5 minutes per side until desired doneness. Let it rest, then slice thinly against the grain.
  2. Prepare the Tortillas: Warm your corn tortillas in a dry skillet for about 30 seconds on each side until soft and slightly charred.
  3. Assemble: Place the sliced steak on the tortillas. Top with finely chopped onions and fresh cilantro.
  4. Serve: Squeeze lime over the tacos for extra flavor, and enjoy!
