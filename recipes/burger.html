Burger

description:

A juicy, perfectly seared beef patty, topped with melted cheese, fresh lettuce, ripe tomato, crispy pickles, and a tangy sauce, all sandwiched between a soft, toasted bun. Classic, satisfying, and packed with flavor in every bite.

Ingredients:

  • Beef patties
  • Buns
  • Onion
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Pickles
  • cheese of choice
  • sauce(s) of choice

Steps:

  1. Grill a beef patty to your desired doneness. Toast a bun and melt cheese on the patty. Assemble with lettuce, tomato, pickles, and your favorite sauce. Serve and enjoy!.
